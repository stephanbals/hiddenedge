HiddenEdge – Hiring Decision Engine

How HiddenEdge Works

Step 1 — Upload your CV
Upload your CV in DOCX or PDF format.

Step 2 — Paste Job Description
Provide the job description you want to evaluate.

Step 3 — Nestor (Decision Engine)
Nestor analyzes your CV against the job and determines:
• Fit score
• Strengths
• Gaps
• Risk indicators
• Whether you should apply

Step 4 — Alec (CV Optimization)
Alec generates an optimized version of your CV specifically tailored to the job, improving your chances.

Why Two Entities?
Nestor provides objective decision support.
Alec focuses on execution and improvement.
This separation ensures clarity between analysis and action.

Free vs Paid Usage

HiddenEdge allows you to preview your CV analysis and the optimized CV for free.

Downloading the tailored CV requires an upgrade.

This way, you can first evaluate the quality before deciding to unlock full functionality.
